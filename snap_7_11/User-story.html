As a primary user, I can import images into an email campaign builder of Mail Chimp.

Lola started a baking and lifestyle blog and wants to insert images of her new baking creations into email posts to engage with her followers.

  • Considering having another child sometime within the next year
  • creating an online boutique business with engaging in social media- primarily using instagram for picture of her baked goods
  • also developing her photography skills to take pictures of her family and food
  • shops frequently at whole foods and buys organic
  • has her own chickens who lay all the eggs for her baking
